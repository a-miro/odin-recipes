Creamy Pasta Sauce with Fresh Herbs

Description

This recipe started out as a "Creamy Pasta Sauce with Fresh Herbs" from the Silver Palate Cookbook but has morphed over the years into a beast of my own making.

Ingredients

  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 4 tbsp salted butter
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 pinch cayenne
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pack (8 oz) smoked salmon, chopped
  • 2 tbsp dried dill
  • 3 packs (2 lbs) Trader Joe's Spinach & Chive Linguine

Steps

  1. Combine the cream, butter, salt, nutmeg, and cayenne in a heavy saucepan.
  2. Simmer for at least 15 minutes or until sauce is reduced and thickened.
  3. While the sauce thickens, fill a large pasta pot with water, salt it, and bring it to a boil.
  4. Whisk in the parmesan, salmon, and dill and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  5. Taste and correct seasonings. Serve immediately.
